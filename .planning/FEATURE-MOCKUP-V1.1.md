## Key Differentiators

1. **AI Decision Transparency** - See exactly why AI did what it did
2. **Predictive Analytics** - Churn risk, upsell opportunities, likely next actions
3. **Health/Learning Tracking** - "+15 learned today", trend detection
4. **Smart Recommendations** - AI suggests who to reach out to, when to escalate
5. **Quality Measurement** - Every response scored on quality/speed/helpfulness
6. **Sentiment Progression** - Watch customer mood change in real-time
7. **Risk Detection** - Automatically flag risky/angry/churning customers
8. **Knowledge Integration** - Show which KB articles AI is using
9. **Customer Intelligence** - VIP detection, value scoring, behavior predictions
10. **Seamless Takeover** - One-click pause/resume, no context loss
